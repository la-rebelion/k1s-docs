# Sign up

:::info

The best way to learn Kubernetes is by doing, and K1s, a Kubernetes serverless cluster will help you quickly get to grips with the technology removing the tough process of installation and configuration.

:::

To sign up for K1s:

Navigate to https://console.k1s.me

1. If you do not have an invite code to access the K1s Technical Preview, click <Highlight color="#25c2a0">[Request early access](htts://k1s.me/early-access.html?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=early_access&utm_content=getting_start)</Highlight>.
2. On the K1s Technical Preview page, enter your email address and click Request Early Access. An invite code will be sent to your email address.
3. In the invite email, click on the invite link, which directs you to the K1s sign-in page.
4. Sign in with an email or Google account.

After signing in, you are directed to the K1s Console where you can create your first project. For instructions, see Setting up a project.
